About Me

Hello, my name is Alan Butcher, I am a junior full stack developer, MBA graduate, and computer science student. I began coding as a hobby two years ago in-between my college semesters. I am really drawn to the challenge of building something from nothing, and leveraging existing technologies to business solutions. I have attended a three-month, full-time, full-stack, coding bootcamp, and after graduating with my MBA this spring, I enrolled in a computer science program to pursue a professional career in development. When I am not coding, or in school, I enjoy playing video games, hiking, exploring, and going on adventures with my family!

Front end

I have experience using React, NextJS, JavaScript, Flexbox, Bootstrap, Vue.js and Vuex for creating single page applications. I am always interested in current UI/UX design to create beautiful applications that maximize usability and user experience.

Back end

Experienced in Node.js with an Express framework, Mongoose, MongoDB, C#, MySQL, creating REST API's, Object Oriented Programming and SOLID principles.

Business

Projects
